VMLauncher 2.0

A modern application based on Material Design to ease your work with the GibbiX.
Machines

Manage your virtual machines. Not only the default ones! You can easily import custom machines, start, configure or repare them.
Backups

Create backups to save your data. Furthermore it is a possibility to share your machines if you did any special configuration or great work!
Sessions

Gather your most used machines in a session. Add or remove machines whenever you want. And start them all with just one click!
